Ham Sandwich Recipe

Ingredients

For the herb mayo

  • 1 c. mayonnaise
  • 2 tbsp. freshly chopped parsley
  • 2 tsp. freshly chopped thyme
  • 1 tbsp. extra-virgin olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced

For the sandwich

  • 8 slices crusty bread, such as sourdough or ciabatta
  • 1/2 c. herb mayo
  • 1/4 c. Dijon mustard
  • 12 slices deli ham
  • 8 slices provolone
  • 1 arugula
  • 1 tomato, thinly sliced
  • 1/2 red onion, thinly sliced

Instructions

  1. In a medium bowl whisk together mayonnaise, parsley, thyme, oil, and garlic.
  2. Spread 2 tablespoons herb mayo on 4 slices of bread. Spread 1 tablespoon mustard on the other 4 slices of bread. Top each mayo slice of bread with 3 slices of ham, 2 slices of provolone, arugula, a couple slices of tomato, and red onion. Top with remaining bread slices, mustard side down.
